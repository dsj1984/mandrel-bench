## Autonomy guardrail (mandrel arm)

mandrel-vs-control delta (Epic #66, Story #77): the bare control arm’s
zero-intervention baseline is defined, not measured, so a delta against

finding.

## Plan-vs-deliver attribution (mandrel arm)

Each mandrel-arm run crosses its intrinsic PLAN quality with the delivered
OUTCOME and plan-adherence (D-019 §3.4), attributing a result to the plan
phase vs the deliver phase rather than lumping both into one number.
Crossing all three inputs is the Goodhart backstop — a plan cannot read as
“working as intended” without a matching outcome.

## Recommended improvements

### 🔴 High — Add a ceremony-lite path for trivial scopes

- **Evidence:** hello-world overhead floor ≈ 3287583 tokens / $3.3748 above control (quality gain 0) — a positive floor with no matching quality gain.
- **Action:** Gate the full /plan→/deliver ceremony behind a complexity threshold so trivial scopes skip the planning/decomposition tax that buys no quality here.

### 🔴 High — Recalibrate the difficulty ladder or the instrument

- **Evidence:** [calibration] overheadRatio.tokenRatio did not fall: hello-world=0.34491152941103664 ≤ story-scope=0.36432342499173925
- **Action:** Efficiency did not rise and/or overhead ratio did not fall across the ladder. Re-grade the scenario difficulties or widen the gap between rungs so the instrument is sensitive to scaling.
